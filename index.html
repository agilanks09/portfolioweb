About Me

👋 Hey There!

I'm Agilan KS, a self-taught designer 🎨 driven by passion ❤️. Starting on this adventure in 2018, I've been fueled by a love for crafting intuitive, user-friendly designs 👥. Recently, I completed my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction from the University College of Dublin 🎓, refining my skills at the intersection of technology and user experience 💻🤝.

As a digital product designer, I've played a crucial role in bringing the ideas of 5+ startups to life 🚀, from my early days at GUVI Geek Network, where I contributed to creating user-friendly e-learning experiences 📚, to Manta Solution in Hong Kong, immersing myself in the Fintech sector 💹, and more recently, as a consultant at Totality Platform, navigating the unique challenges of the hospitality industry 🏨.

Beyond my work, I'm passionate about art 🖌️ and staying active 🏃. In design, I love diving into AI 🧠, design systems, user research 🔍, and gamification 🎲. These areas boost my skills and keep me excited, almost creating cool and fun digital stuff! 🌟
